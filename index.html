Troubleshooting Tips

If you're experiencing issues, try the following steps:

  • Check your internet connection: Ensure you have a stable internet connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account, go to your profile page, and click on "Edit Profile." You can update your information there.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account, go to your profile page, and click on "Delete Account." You can delete your account there.

What should I do if I encounter a bug?

If you encounter a bug, please let us know by contacting support. Provide as much detail as possible, including steps to reproduce the issue.
